Do's and Don'ts

  • Ration ink blue to interaction: one primary button per region, selection, focus.
  • Let the PDF page be the brightest, most contrasted object on screen.
  • Snap every dimension to the 4px grid; every string to the voice guide.
  • Use borders and surface shifts for hierarchy; only pages and modals cast shadows.
  • Show state with the chip vocabulary — color-coded, terse, lowercase.
  • No gradients, glassmorphism, glows, or decorative illustration.
  • No celebration: no confetti, animated checkmarks, or exclamation marks.
  • No onboarding chrome: no tours, first-run tooltips, or "pro tip" cards.
  • No new colors — a need for one means the need is misdiagnosed.
  • No motion beyond 120ms color/opacity eases.
  • No CDN assets in the app — fonts and icons ship bundled or not at all.
